About Us

We are Dante Valentino
and Jay Green.

Two creative forces united by a shared obsession: making brands unforgettable.

With decades of combined experience across design, photography, videography, fashion, tech, and brand consulting, we don't just follow trends. We create markets where none existed. Our backgrounds include work with global giants like Google, NBC, SyFy, PepsiCo, Ducati, and DJI. But our strength isn't just in our résumés. It's in our vision.

At our core, we are creative consultants who specialize in building brands that feel alive. Whether we're crafting visual stories, directing campaign strategy, or developing a magnetic social presence, we bring relentless originality, cinematic aesthetics, and sharp business instincts to every project.

We know how to move fast, think boldly, and execute with precision. Jay brings unmatched speed, fashion instinct, and visual intensity. Dante brings emotional intelligence, narrative strategy, and the ability to translate abstract ideas into vivid, seductive design. Together, we don't just make things look good. We make them work.

Our mission is simple: To help creators, companies, and dreamers stand out — loud, proud, and unforgettable.
