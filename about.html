Our Story

We don't follow trends.
We create markets.

We are Dante Valentino and Jay Green — two creative forces united by a shared obsession: making brands unforgettable.

With decades of combined experience across design, photography, videography, fashion, tech, and brand consulting, we don't just follow trends. We create markets where none existed.

Our backgrounds include work with global giants like Google, NBC, SyFy, PepsiCo, Ducati, and DJI. But our strength isn't just in our résumés. It's in our vision.

At our core, we are creative consultants who specialize in building brands that feel alive. Whether we're crafting visual stories, directing campaign strategy, or developing a magnetic social presence, we bring relentless originality, cinematic aesthetics, and sharp business instincts to every project.

We know how to move fast, think boldly, and execute with precision. Together, we don't just make things look good. We make them work.

Our mission is simple: to help creators, companies, and dreamers stand out — loud, proud, and unforgettable.
